MAPS EXPLORER

You will find a variety of maps. You can let them study which movement you are going for

Blizzard Battle

It is unlocked when all 120 Gold Cards have been collected and all six worlds have been cleared in the Story Mode.

Field of Grass

It is a valley in which the bottom is covered in thick grass. The grass comes up to about the middle of a Bomberman, and almost completely conceals normal bombs.

Greedy Trap

Eight Soft Blocks are in the small area in the center of the screen, on the lowest floor. A narrow path surrounds it on the middle floor

In the Gutter

It is unlocked when all six worlds of the Story Mode have been cleared. The stage is covered in a stream of water, which flows to the left or right, carrying bombs and players with the flow.

Lost at Sea

It is unlocked when all 120 Gold Cards have been collected and all six worlds have been cleared in the Story Mode.

Pyramid

It takes place on top of a large pyramid surrounded by water. All players start next to each other at the top of the map. Sixteen Soft Blocks appear at the bottom of the map at the onset of the stage.

Rock Garden

It is a flat, simple field stage with five pillars and sixteen Soft Blocks. Due to its simplicity, it is a recommended stage for beginning players.

Sea Sick

It is unlocked when all six worlds of the Story Mode have been cleared. The stage is a pirate ship on stormy seas, slowly swaying back and forth, around and around.
