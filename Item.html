Finally, you can see all the items you need to use to beat your opponents. Study them carefully what they are and how you will use them at the right time.

Bombs

The Bomb is usually the player's sole method of attack in the Bomberman series.

Fire Bomb

Fire Bombs are the most common bomb in the series. In the original Bomberman game it fires in four directions, however in later games they have been known to explode in a wider range.

READ MORE

Ice Bomb

The Ice Bomb is a bomb made of ice. It can be used to freeze water spouts to either create bridges or stop objects associated with the water.

READ MORE

Wind Bomb

Wind Bomb (sometimes known as a Hurricane Bomb) is a type of bomb that uses air. It's either green or yellow in color and sometimes has fan blades rotating along the top of it. Other times it has air circulating around it.

READ MORE

Water Bomb

The Water Bomb, as its name suggests is a bomb filled with water. When it explodes, the bomb will release the water inside onto whatever is nearby. It's generally used to extinguish fires.

READ MORE

Thunder Bomb

The Thunder Bomb is a type of bomb that can send out a blast of electricity. It is also known as the Lightning Bomb in some cases. A lightning bolt is represented in the bomb to indicate it as a thunder bomb.

READ MORE

Navarm Bomb

The Navarm Bomb is a bomb that sprays lava in four directions. The lava then becomes pillars of fire for a few seconds.

READ MORE

Light Bomb

The Light Bomb is a bomb that creates a dome of light when it explodes. It can stop time for anything inside the dome as well as doing damage.

READ MORE

Gravity Bomb

The Gravity Bomb (also known as the Shadow Bomb) is a bomb that creates a powerful black hole. It will suck in nearby enemies, objects, and items (and even the player if not careful) and destroy them.

POWER-UPS

Power-Ups are items that affect the abilities of a player during gameplay.

Extra Bomb

Increases the maximum number of bombs that can be laid on the ground by one. (Maximum ten)

Explosion Expander

Increases the range of explosions when bombs are detonated by one level. (Maximum ten)

Accelerator

Increases the speed at which Bomberman moves by one level. (Decreased by one level each time a life is lost)

Remote Control

Allows bombs to be detonated by remote control.

Maximum Explosion

Increases the range of explosions to maximum.

Kick

Allows the ability to kick bombs.

Boxing Glove

Allows the ability to punch bombs and send them flying.

Skull

Temporarily reduces speed, Radically increases speed, Lays bombs randomly, Disallows laying bombs, Makes Bomberman invisible, Keeps laying bombs at high speed, Allows only one minimum-range bomb to be laid at a time.

OTHER ITEMS

Indestructible Armor

When Bomberman wears Indestructible Armor, he flashes for a set length of time during which he is unaffected by explosions or attacks from enemies.

Bomberman

Increases the stock of Bombermen by one.

Bomb Passer

Allows Bomberman to pass through bombs laid on the ground.

Block Passer

Allows Bomberman to pass over Soft Blocks (destructable walls). Very powerful in clearing the level while invulnerable.

Super Bomb

Allows bomb explosions to pass through obstacles such as enemies and walls.

Time

Restores the timer to the counter setting the stage began on.

Fire Extinguisher

Increases the number of Bombermen in stock to maximum and increases player's score.

Heart

Bomberman takes 2 hits to lose a life instead of one.

Mystery Item

The effects of this item are a mystery until you pick it up. (actually just adds points to your score, number of remaining enemies X 1000)

Rice Ball

Increases the player's score.

Cup and Ball

Increases the player's score.

Cake

Increases the player's score.

Ice Cream Cone

Increases the player's score.

Apple

Increases the player's score.

Frozen Pop

Increases the player's score.
