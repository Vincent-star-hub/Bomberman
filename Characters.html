Pick Your Character

All the Characters are all here. Study them to learn more!

Bomberman

White Bomber

“ JUSTICE WILL PREVAIL! ”
“ Wait for your chance ”

White is the group's "big brother," a dedicated yet sad individual. His zeal
for justice is contagious, but his
siblings don't share it, and it typically goes to waste. White, stays
undeterred in his fight against evil.

Black Bomber

“ Fell in love with me again, did ya?
I'm such a heartbreaker... ”

Vain and narcissistic to the point that
it's a little embarrassing, Black Bomber tries to act the cool pragmatist, but all
he really excels at is coming up with excuses for why he hasn't worked
hard enough.

Pink Bomber

“ Just come to me if there's anything
you want to talk about! No, really... I insist! ”

She's a bit of a nosy to other people's business, but if
she finds someone in need, she has
no choice but to assist them.

Blue Bomber

“ Ahhh, I'm tired out! I really need to
get some...Zzzz.... ”
“ I wish I could sleep thirty hours a
day. ”

Blue's unique power is to fall asleep whenever, anywhere, and for as long
as he needs at any time of day or
night.

Red Bomber

“ Fine by me! I can bomb all day,
buddy ”

Red is a fiery-tempered man who
aspires to be a hero. In his own way,
he admires White Bomber and, like
his older brother. His first reaction is
to fix every problem by detonating a large bomb.

Yellow Bomber

“ Oh, a butterfly! Wheee! Ahaha... ”
“ Oh, look it's a UFO. ”

Yellow is naïve, innocent, and strange. He's always lost in his own world, no matter how bad the Bomberman Bros.

Aqua Bomber

“ Stop, violence never got anyone anywhere... ”

Aqua is a sweet, pleasant, and
peaceful young lady who despises all forms of violence. Her one sincere
wish is for both her friends and foes
to lay down their weapons and stop fighting.

Green Bomber

“ See? Don't think you can mess with
me just 'cause I'm the youngest! ”

Everyone's favorite little brother is
Green. He's so adorable that
everything he does makes the others
want to embrace and protect him, and none of them can ever say no to one
of his requests.

Attack Type

Simon Belmont Bomber

Uses the Vampire Killer to pull in opponents, even into bottomless pits.

Raiden Bomber

Uses Ninja Run to perform dash
strikes. When used against opponents, they will be knocked back away
from you.

Anubis Bomber

Uses Zero Shift which allows him to
swap positions with opponents that
are in front of them, even if
they're eight square feet away.

Dracula Bomber

Uses Dark Curse, which sets up to
four invisible dark circles anywhere
on the map. Once a bomb is placed
on a dark circle, the bomb will move away from that position.

Goemon Bomber

Uses the Oedo Blaster to place all of
your bombs in a single row.

Riou Bomber

Uses Twin Fang to blast characters
and bombs in his area away with his tonfas.

Pyramid Head Bomber

Uses the Final Judgement to one hit
k.o. opponents. The downside is that
this move can also slow you down.

Soma Cruz Bomber

Uses the Power of Dominance, Soma seals and temporarily copies the
special abilities of characters who
come into contact with his bats.

Speed Type

Vic Viper Bomber

Uses Speed Up to boost straight
forward on the map. The downside
being that you go in one direction,
and you don't stop until you hit an
object or map wall.

Shiori Fujisaki
Bomber

Uses the Tokimeki Field, a love circle
that stops nearby opponents dead in
their tracks for a few seconds.

Jehuty Bomber

Uses Zero Shift A which allows him to swap positions with opponents that
are in front of them, even if they're
eight square feet away.

Old Snake Bomber

Uses the Sneaking technique. This
allows him to turn completely invisible and be undetected by opponents momentarily.

Lance Bomber

Uses the Contra Burst that shoots 8 square feet in front of him. This causes opponents to be stunned, and bombs
to instantly explode.

Bill Bomber

Uses the Contra Shot that shoots four square feet in front of him. This
causes opponents to become
stunned, and bombs to disappear.

Alucard Bomber

He can transform into a ball of Mist
for about one second. During this
time,he is invulnerable to explosions
and hazards, and can pass through bombs. His movement speed does
not change. When the effect ends, he pauses in place to revert to his
base form.

Naked Snake
Bomber

Uses Sneaking technique.
He becomes completely invisible for
ten seconds. If he collects an item, however, the effect will still appear around his body.
